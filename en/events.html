December 2nd, 2023

Christmas Concert - piano, violin & voice recitals.

The Chicago Romanian American Choir coordinated by Mrs. Nicoleta Roman (Chicago Symphony Orchestra Lyric Artist) will perform a recital of Christmas Carols and National Anthems. Guests of Seara Culturala Romaneasca will also perform recitals of classical music and Christmas Carols. Come join us at Naperville Public Library (3015 Cedar Glade Dr, Naperville, IL 60564) starting 2.00 pm to 4.00 pm and enjoy beautiful relaxing music. Refreshments and traditional Romanian food will be provided by hosts and volunteers. As usual, our artists will receive gifts from Santa. Looking forward to seeing you!
