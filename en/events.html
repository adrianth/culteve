November 22th, 2019

The Colors of Fall Season

- poetry & music recitals-


Friday, November 22th, 2019, 6-9 pm, "Seara Culturala Romaneasca" invites families and friends to join us -

"Seara Culturala Romaneasca" will take place at Naperville Public Library, 3015 Cedar Glade Dr., Naperville. If you want to participate with an artistic recital, visit our Facebook page, or contact us at the phone number provided on our web-site.

1. Introduction & brief theme presentation;

2. Poetry recitals: The Fall Seasons of our childhood;

3. Youth's music recitals: violin

"Caroll of the Bells" - Johnny & Lucas Raicu;

"You raise me up" - Mara Theodorescu;

4. Story time: slideshow projection of "Amintiri din Copilarie" by Ion Creanga;

5. Socializing, taking pictures, a quick snack, coffee & cake.

Backround sound and visual presentation of Fall paintings (Alexandru Darida, Nicolae Grigorescu, etc.) & photos by Alexandra & Marie Theodorescu

January 26th, 2018

Celebrating Romanian's National Poet, Mihai Eminescu

Remembering Eminescu's poetry and prose, "Cultural Evening" celebrated 168 years since the Greatest Romanian National Poet was born. .

1) A visual presentation on Eminescu's professional activity and personal life by Maria Theodorescu

2) "Luceafarul" presentation with an accurate english translation - pr.dr.Rodica Baranescu

3) Music recital - Andrei Olaru

4) Open discussions and shared opinions on poetry and prose authors, Eminescu's inspiration sources and the social conditions and cultural environment in which he created.
