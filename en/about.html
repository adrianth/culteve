Marie Theodorescu, founder of Art & Culture Chapter, and Art & Culture Director; Educational management professional in Naperville, Il, with a Master's degree in Management & Administration. In her free time, Marie paints landscapes and icons; to improve her painting technique she took painting classes in Bucharest (Romania) and Wheaton, Il. Besides teaching & advocating as school liaison within PD Advisory Council in School District 204, she is actively involved in different local cultural projects within Chicago Area. In the last couple of years she coordonated icon painting classes and Sunday School classes within local Christian Churches. Currently, Marie manages multicultural World Cafe projects, held periodically in District 204 schools & "Romanian Cultural Evening" events series, held at Naperville Public Library.
