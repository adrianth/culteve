Maria Theodorescu, co-founder of Art & Culture SPOC chapter and Art & Culture Committee Director; Maria is a teacher and NCLB paraprofessional educator in Naperville, Il. She graduated Economy & Business Law University with a Master's degree in Management & Administration within the Academy of Economic Studies of Bucharest. Besides her teaching and professional business administration trainer career, Maria continued to develop her artistic skills by taking painting classes within Art School of Bucharest. She joined icon painting classes held by a Christian Orthodox church in Wheaton, Il, improving her wood icon painting technique. At present, Maria is coordinating "Romanian Cultural Evening" series and icon painting classes, organizing monthly cultural events within the local community at Naperville Public Library. You can read about her activity in "Sighisorean Crenels".

Ligia Grindeanu, co-founder of Art & Culture SPOC chapter and Peotry & Creative Writing Committee Director within the organization; Ligia is a pediatrician in Chicago, Il and a poet. She is a member of the poetry workshop "ND Cocea Anotimpuri" from her hometown, Sighisoara, co-founder of the Romanian Cultural Organization from Iowa City, including the poetry workshop "Dor", and member of "Retro" cenacle of Chicago. Her poetry was published in several cultural publications such as: "Free word", "Fortress voice", "Ambassador", "Sighisorean Crenels", "Seasons", "The eternity of a second" etc. Ligia is involved in many cultural activities organized by the Romanian cultural community from Chicago area. In 2003 She published a poetry volume called "Beyong silence" and she is working on a her new volum called "Seasons of longing", almost ready to be published.

Daniela Duta, Public Relations Director within SPOC's Art & Culture Committee; Daniela is a broker and a referenced professional realtor; she joined the team due to her unconditional support and implication in cultural and social activities organized by the local community. Daniela graduated Business Administration at Craiova University and developed her business career in Chicago area. She is constantly involved in her community social and cultural activities, supporting and connecting people with the same interests and affinities. Daniela is a reliable volunteer for any charity activities organized by her local community; she initiated and organized fundraisers for orphans and children in need, constantly seeking and finding the right solution to help her community. She joined the SPOC's Art & Culture Committee because she finds joy in art, shared traditions and cultures but also because it supports a nobile cause as helping orphans.
