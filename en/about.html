Marie Theodorescu, co-founder of Art & Culture SPOC Chapter and Art & Culture Committee Director; Educational management professional in Naperville, Il, with a Master's degree in Management & Administration; in her free time, Marie paints landscapes and icons. Besides her main activities within 204 School District (teaching & advocating as PD Advisory Council member & school liaison), she is involved and participates in many cultural projects. She took painting classes (in Bucharest and Wheaton). In the last couple of years she coordonated icon painting classes and Sunday School classes within local Christian Churches. Currently, Marie manages multicultural events in Chicago Area such as: the most recent - since 2019, The World Cafe cultural school project & since 2017, "Romanian Cultural Evening" events series, held at Naperville Public Library.

Ligia Grindeanu, co-founder of Art & Culture SPOC Chapter and Poetry & Creative Writing Committee Director; Ligia is a pediatrician in Chicago, Il, and a poet. She is a member of the poetry workshop "ND Cocea Anotimpuri" from her hometown, Sighisoara, co-founder of the Romanian Cultural Organization from Iowa City, including the poetry workshop "Dor", and member of "Retro" cenacle of Chicago. Her poetry was published in several cultural publications such as: "Free word", "Fortress voice", "Ambassador", "Sighisorean Crenels", "Seasons", "The eternity of a second" etc. Ligia is involved in many cultural activities organized by the Romanian cultural community from Chicago area. In 2003 she published a poetry volume called "Beyong silence" and she is working on a her new volum called "Seasons of longing".
