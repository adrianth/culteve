What

A place to find ourselves and others through poetry, prose, music, dance and painting/drawing. A place to remember traditions, poets and writers. A place to enjoy art and culture in all it’s forms and to meet people that share the same interests. Cultural evening is the place where, through art and culture activities, we understand our individual and shared history, we discuss and share art and cultural experiences, as we consider them chronicles of our own lives or the lives of others.

When

We meet periodically (October through May), and we enjoy poetry, prose, music recitals, painting/drawing/photography presentations, we share traditions, life stories and historical events of places/countries throughout history. The meetings ussualy take place at the Naperville Public Library.

On specific occasions (Mother’s day, International Children’s day etc., Christmas), we meet in different locations, depending on the event. If you are interested to participate with your artistic contribution or if you enjoy art and culture in general, contact us through e-mail. You will be anounced privately about future events, or visit Naperville Public Library's events public facebook page.

Who

Marie Theodorescu is an Education Professional in Glenview & Naperville, IL. Besides teaching and advocating within Glenbrook School District 225 & Naperville School District 204, Marie managed local World Cafe multicultural events, in her role as member of PDAC Advisory Council. The main goal is to address equity, diversity and inclusion in schools and among community members. At present, she is involved in several cultural projects organized at Naperville Public Library or/and within local churches: icon painting classes, Sunday School classes, and "Romanian Cultural Evening" event series. In her free time, Marie paints landscapes and icons. Although due to COVID-19, public gatherings are currently postponed, Marie will continue to collaborate with Naperville Public Library in order to organize & promote cultural events in the nearest future.
