Marie Theodorescu, Founder of Art & Culture Chapter; School District 204 Education professional in Naperville, Il, with a Master's degree in Management & Administration, Marie is teaching and advocating as K12 guest teacher and school liaison within PD Advisory Council. In her free time, Marie paints landscapes and icons; to improve her painting technique she took painting classes in Bucharest (Romania) and Wheaton, Il. She is actively involved in different cultural projects within Chicago Area. In the last couple of years she coordonated icon painting classes and Sunday School classes within local Christian Churches. At present, Marie manages multicultural World Cafe projects, held periodically in District 204 schools & "Romanian Cultural Evening" events series, held at Naperville Public Library. Also, currently, Marie is writing her first book-guide for the young generations but not only, to be released by the end of 2020.
