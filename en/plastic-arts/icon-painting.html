Icon painting

The icons displayed are painted by Calina Boiciuc, Mirela Kulcsar and Maria Theodorescu.

Icon painting workshops We are excited to be hosting a workshop on alternating icon painting on both wood and glass surfaces. This unique workshop provide the opportunity to learn traditional techniques of icon painting, and to apply these techniques to both wood and glass surfaces. If you're interested in joining us for this workshop, please contact us to reserve your spot. We will provide you with all the necessary materials and guidance needed to create beautiful, one-of-a-kind icons. This is a great opportunity to learn a new skill and create something truly special.
