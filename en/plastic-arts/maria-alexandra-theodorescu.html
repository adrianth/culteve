Maria Alexandra Theodorescu

Ally Th personal site & blog - Maria Alexandra Theodorescu, DePaul University Student majoring in Computer Science with Software Development Concentration. In her free time, Ally loves to paint and draw; she made her artistic debut through Naperville Fine Art Gallery in 2016. Since then, Ally is participating with relevant artistic work in local art exhibitions, through school or independently. In 2018, some of her art work was published in "Creneluri Sighisorene"- East European Cultural Publication - Young Artists section. In 2019, Ally received "The Pursuit of Excellence" Award, Dupage County. The drawings displayed are from her highschool years 2015-2019. At present she is mainly focused on school and research, but painting and drawing remain her primarly hobbies.
