Maria Alexandra Theodorescu

Ally Th personal site & blog - Maria Alexandra Theodorescu, DePaul University Student, majoring in Computer Science with Software Development Concentration. In her free time, Ally loves to paint and draw, some of her work was published in 2018 in "Creneluri Sighisorene", an European cultural publication. Her artistic work was recognized in 2019 by the Art Department from NVHS & Dupage County, granting her "The Pursuit of Excellence Award", after 4 years of highschool.
