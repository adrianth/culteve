Maria Alexandra Theodorescu

Ally Th personal site & blog - Maria Alexandra Theodorescu, DePaul University Student, majoring in Computer Science with Software Development Concentration. In her free time, Ally loves to paint and draw; she made her artistic debut through Naperville Fine Art Gallery in 2016. She continued to participate with relevant artistic work in local art exhibitions, through school or independently. In 2018, some of her art work was published in "Creneluri Sighisorene"- East European Cultural Publication - Young Artists section. Her artistic work was recognized in 2019 by the Art Department from NVHS & by Dupage County, granting her "The Pursuit of Excellence Award". Although at present, she is focused on school work, Ally continues to paint and draw in her spare time, continuously improving her artistic skills and techniques.
