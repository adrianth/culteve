Maria Alexandra Theodorescu

Ally Th personal site & blog - Maria Alexandra (Ally) Theodorescu, DePaul University Student - majoring in Computer Science with a Software Development Concentration and a Data Science minor - in her free time also passionate about painting, drawing, and photography. Ally made her artistic debut through Naperville Fine Art Gallery in 2016, where si had displayed her personal online art gallery for almost 4 years. Since then, Ally is participating with relevant artistic work in local art exhibitions in Chicago Area. In 2018, some of her drawing and paintings were published in "Creneluri Sighisorene"- An East European Cultural Publication - Young Artists section. In 2019, Ally received "The Pursuit of Excellence" Award of Dupage County, from NVHS Art Department, for her overall artistic contribution. The drawings, paintings and photos displayed here are from her highschool years 2015-2019. At present, she is mainly focused on school work and biomedical research, but painting, drawing and photography remain her primarily hobbies.
