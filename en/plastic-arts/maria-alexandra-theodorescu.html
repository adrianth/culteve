Maria Alexandra Theodorescu

Ally Th personal site & blog - Maria Alexandra (Ally) Theodorescu, DePaul University Student - majoring in Computer Science with a Software Development Concentration and a Data Science minor - in her free time she enjoys painting, drawing, and photography. Ally made her artistic debut at Naperville Fine Art Gallery exhibition in 2016. Since then, she had displayed her personal online art gallery for 4 years. In 2018, some of her drawing and paintings were published in "Creneluri Sighisorene"- An East European Cultural Publication - Young Artists section. In 2019, Ally received "The Pursuit of Excellence" Award of Dupage County, from Neuqua Valley Hight School's Art Department, for her overall artistic contribution. The drawings, paintings and photos displayed here are from 2015 to 2019. At present, she is mainly focused on school work and biomedical research, but painting, drawing and photography remain her primarily hobbies.
