Handmade candles

Starting from March 2021, we will be hosting handmade candle workshops. If you're interested in joining us for a fun and informative session, please follow our Facebook page to get more information and updates. Our workshops will provide you with an opportunity to learn the art of candle-making, and create your very own unique candles. We will guide you through the process of selecting and blending fragrances, and teach you the techniques of candle-making that will enable you to produce beautiful candles that you can take home with you. Stay tuned for more details on our Facebook page!

Handmade candles workshops During our handmade candle workshops, we will be using soy wax as the primary wax for container candles, and blended waxes for pillar and molded candles. In terms of decorations, we will be providing a variety of options, including dry flowers and leaves, as well as semi-precious stones. We will also be using natural oils for fragrance, ensuring that the scents of our candles are both high-quality and environmentally friendly. We look forward to sharing our passion for candle-making with you and creating beautiful, personalized candles together.
