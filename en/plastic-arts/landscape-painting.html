Landscape painting




Landscape painting workshops Our upcoming events will take place outdoors from April to June, and we have chosen Morton Arboretum and Phillips Park Zoo as our preferred locations. By holding our events in these beautiful outdoor spaces, we hope to provide a unique and enjoyable experience for all attendees. We encourage everyone to come out and enjoy the natural beauty of these locations while participating in our events. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, animal lover, or simply looking for a fun activity to do with friends and family, join us!
