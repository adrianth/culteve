Alina Gilice

Alina's personal website

Alina Gilice, born on September 16th, 1974 in Bucharest, Romania, has emerged as a self-made artist since 2012. Alina heeded her inner calling and embarked on a journey of self-expression through her unique form of art. For Alina, painting is an adventure, and her canvases are the only place where she feels completely free. It's inspiring to see someone pursue their passion for art and create their own style of expression. Alina's dedication to her craft is evident in her work, and it's admirable that she has found a way to channel her creativity into something that brings her joy and fulfillment. Her love for painting shines through in every stroke of her brush, and it's clear that she has a deep appreciation for the beauty and power of art.
