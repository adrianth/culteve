Alina Gilice

Alina's personal website

Alina Gilice was born in Bucharest, Romania, September 16th, 1974. Growing into self made artist since 2012, she followed her inner call by creating an unique form of artistic expression. Painting is an adventure for her, and the only place Alina feels completely free is on her canvases.
